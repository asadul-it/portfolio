💡 Tips for Best Results

  • 🎯 High Contrast: Images with clear difference between subject and background work best
  • 📸 Good Lighting: Well-lit photos improve accuracy significantly
  • ✨ Simple Backgrounds: Solid or uniform backgrounds are easier to remove
  • 👤 Portraits & Products: Works excellently with people and product photos
  • 🎨 Hair & Details: For complex edges (hair, fur), increase sensitivity slider
  • ⚙️ Adjust Sensitivity: Lower value (10-30) for simple backgrounds, higher (50-80) for complex
  • 🖼️ Image Quality: Higher resolution images produce better results

⚡ Pro Tips:

  • White/light backgrounds → Keep sensitivity low (20-40)
  • Complex/colorful backgrounds → Increase sensitivity (50-80)
  • If too much removed → Decrease sensitivity
  • If background remains → Increase sensitivity
