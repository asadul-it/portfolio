Understanding DNS Records

A Record

Maps domain names to IPv4 addresses. Essential for directing traffic to your server.

AAAA Record

Maps domain names to IPv6 addresses. The next-generation internet protocol.

MX Record

Specifies mail servers for the domain. Critical for email delivery.

NS Record

Identifies authoritative nameservers for the domain.

TXT Record

Stores text information. Used for verification and security policies.

CNAME Record

Creates an alias for another domain name. Used for subdomains.
