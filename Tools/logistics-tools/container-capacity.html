Container Capacity Optimization Guide

Maximize your container loading efficiency with these proven strategies and industry standards for container capacity utilization.

Container Standards

  • 20ft Standard: 1,172 cu ft, 48,000 lbs max
  • 40ft Standard: 2,387 cu ft, 59,200 lbs max
  • 40ft High Cube: 2,660 cu ft, 58,700 lbs max
  • 45ft High Cube: 3,040 cu ft, 63,800 lbs max

Loading Best Practices

  • Aim for 85-90% volume utilization
  • Stagger boxes for stability
  • Use pallets for efficient loading
  • Consider weight distribution

Important Considerations

  • Door opening clearance
  • Weight limits per axle
  • Cargo securing requirements
  • Temperature control needs
