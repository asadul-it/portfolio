Lead Time Calculation Factors

Understand the key factors that influence lead time in logistics and supply chain operations for better planning and efficiency.

Transport Modes

  • Road: 40-80 km/h, affected by traffic
  • Rail: 60-100 km/h, fixed schedules
  • Sea: 20-40 km/h, port delays common
  • Air: 800-900 km/h, fastest but costly

Processing Factors

  • Documentation: 1-3 business days
  • Customs clearance: 2-5 days average
  • Special handling: Adds 1-2 days
  • Weekend/holidays: Add buffer days

Common Delays

  • Weather conditions
  • Port congestion
  • Documentation errors
  • Customs inspections
