VAT Rules & Regulations

VAT Registration

  • Mandatory for businesses with annual turnover exceeding ৳30 lakh (৳3 million)
  • Voluntary registration available for businesses below threshold
  • Must obtain VAT registration certificate from NBR
  • 15-digit BIN (Business Identification Number) issued upon registration
  • Registration must be renewed annually

VAT Invoice Requirements

  • Must contain business name, address, and BIN number
  • Clear description of goods or services provided
  • Separate line items showing VAT amount
  • Sequential invoice numbering required
  • Digital invoices accepted for online transactions

VAT Return Filing

  • Monthly returns must be submitted by 15th of following month
  • Annual VAT return due within 6 months of financial year end
  • Online filing through NBR e-portal mandatory
  • Penalties apply for late submission or non-compliance
  • Maintain records for at least 5 years

Input Tax Credit

  • Registered businesses can claim VAT paid on inputs
  • Credit available only with valid VAT invoices
  • Cannot claim credit on personal expenses
  • Credit must be claimed in same return period
  • Special rules apply for capital goods

Important: VAT regulations are subject to change. Always consult with tax professionals or refer to official NBR guidelines for compliance.
