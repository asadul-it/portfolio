Islamic Prayer Information

Fajr Prayer

The morning prayer before sunrise. Time begins at dawn and ends at sunrise. Consists of 2 Rak'ahs.

Dhuhr Prayer

The noon prayer after the sun passes its zenith. Time begins after Zawal (midday) and lasts until Asr. Consists of 4 Rak'ahs.

Asr Prayer

The afternoon prayer. Time begins when the shadow of an object is equal to its length plus the shadow at noon. Consists of 4 Rak'ahs.

Maghrib Prayer

The evening prayer just after sunset. Time begins immediately after sunset and lasts until twilight disappears. Consists of 3 Rak'ahs.

Isha Prayer

The night prayer. Time begins after twilight disappears and lasts until midnight. Consists of 4 Rak'ahs.

Jumu'ah (Friday)

Friday congregational prayer that replaces Dhuhr prayer. Must be performed in congregation at the mosque.
