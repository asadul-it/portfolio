Important Emergency Guidelines

  • Stay Calm: In any emergency, try to remain calm and speak clearly when calling for help.
  • Provide Location: Always give your exact location, including district, thana, and nearby landmarks.
  • Describe Emergency: Clearly describe the nature of the emergency and the number of people involved.
  • Follow Instructions: Listen carefully to the emergency operator and follow their instructions.
  • Don't Hang Up: Stay on the line until the operator tells you to disconnect.
  • Keep Updated: Save this page and check periodically for updated emergency numbers.
  • Teach Children: Teach children how and when to call emergency services.
  • Know Your Location: Always be aware of your exact address and location details.
