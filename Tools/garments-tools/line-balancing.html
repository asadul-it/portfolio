Calculation Formulas

Manpower Calculation

Manpower = (Total SMV × Target Output) ÷ (Working Hours × 60 × Efficiency)

Calculates the number of operators required to achieve target output.

Daily Capacity

Capacity = (Manpower × Working Hours × 60 × Efficiency) ÷ Total SMV

Maximum output possible with given manpower and efficiency.

Line Efficiency

Efficiency = (Standard Minutes Produced ÷ Actual Minutes Worked) × 100

Percentage of productive time utilization.

Balance Efficiency

Balance % = (Total Station Time ÷ (No. of Stations × Cycle Time)) × 100

Measures how well work is distributed across stations.
