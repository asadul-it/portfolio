Common Wastage Sources

Cutting Room

Improper marker planning, fabric defects, cutting errors, and pattern inefficiencies contribute to 40-60% of total wastage.

45% Average

Sewing Section

Thread waste, rejected pieces, alteration requirements, and stitching errors account for 20-30% of production wastage.

25% Average

Finishing & Packing

Trim waste, packaging material loss, quality rejects, and handling damages contribute to 15-25% of total wastage.

20% Average

Machine & Maintenance

Machine setup waste, maintenance scraps, oil stains, and equipment inefficiencies account for 10-15% of material loss.

12% Average

Wastage Reduction Strategies

Cutting Optimization

  • Implement computerized marker planning
  • Use fabric saving software
  • Optimize pattern placement
  • Train cutting operators regularly
  • Maintain cutting blades sharpness

Production Control

  • Implement lean manufacturing
  • Use automated spreading machines
  • Monitor real-time wastage
  • Establish quality checkpoints
  • Reduce rework and alterations

Waste Management

  • Segregate waste categories
  • Recycle fabric scraps
  • Implement waste tracking system
  • Use waste for sampling
  • Donate usable scraps
