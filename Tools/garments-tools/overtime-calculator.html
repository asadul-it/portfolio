How to Use

  • Enter worker information: name, ID, department, and period
  • Choose calculation method: Monthly Salary or Hourly Rate
  • Input basic salary or hourly rate and working hours
  • Add overtime hours with appropriate rate multiplier (1.5x, 2.0x, etc.)
  • Include holiday/weekend overtime if applicable (usually at higher rate)
  • Add any bonuses, allowances, or deductions
  • Enter absent days to automatically deduct from salary
  • Click "Calculate Salary" to generate detailed payslip
  • Export the payslip for record keeping and payment processing
