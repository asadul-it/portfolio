Uptime & SLA Guidelines

SLA Best Practices

Define clear SLA metrics, monitor continuously, establish escalation procedures, and include penalties/rewards. Regularly review and update SLA agreements based on business needs and technological improvements.

Improving Uptime

Implement redundancy (load balancers, failover systems), regular maintenance schedules, proactive monitoring, automated backups, and disaster recovery plans. Use multiple internet providers and data centers for critical services.

Monitoring & Reporting

Use comprehensive monitoring tools (Nagios, Zabbix, PRTG), implement alerting systems, maintain uptime/downtime logs, generate regular SLA compliance reports, and conduct post-incident reviews for continuous improvement.
