Bandwidth Planning Guidelines

  • Rule of Thumb: Plan for 70% peak utilization to allow for growth and bursts.
  • Quality of Service (QoS): Prioritize voice and video traffic for better performance.
  • Upload vs Download: Most connections have asymmetric speeds (faster download).
  • Overhead: Account for 10-20% protocol overhead in bandwidth calculations.
  • Future Growth: Plan for 25-50% growth over 3 years when sizing connections.
  • Redundancy: Consider redundant connections for critical applications.
