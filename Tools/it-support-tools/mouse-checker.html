Mouse Checker

Test all mouse buttons, scroll wheel, and movement tracking. Click anywhere in the test area to verify your mouse is working properly. Perfect for diagnosing mouse issues!

How to Use

Move your mouse in the test area and click all buttons (left, right, middle). Try scrolling up and down. Working buttons will light up on the visual mouse indicator and show in the statistics.
