Keyboard Checker

Test every key on your keyboard to ensure they're working properly. Press any key to see it light up and hear a click sound. Perfect for diagnosing keyboard issues!

How to Use

Simply press each key on your keyboard. Working keys will light up in green and make a click sound. Keys that don't respond may indicate a hardware issue. Click "Reset Test" to start over or "Generate Report" to see detailed results.
