Understanding Percentage Calculations

Percentage is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100. It's commonly used to compare proportions, calculate discounts, measure changes, and analyze data.

Basic Formulas

  • Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100
  • Part = (Percentage × Whole) / 100
  • Percentage Change = [(New - Old) / Old] × 100
  • Original = Final / (1 + Percentage/100)

Common Applications

  • Discounts and sales calculations
  • Tax calculations (VAT, GST, sales tax)
  • Tip calculations in restaurants
  • Grade calculations and score percentages
  • Financial growth and investment returns

Quick Tips

  • To find 10%: Divide by 10
  • To find 5%: Find 10% and divide by 2
  • To find 1%: Divide by 100
  • To increase by X%: Multiply by (1 + X/100)
  • To decrease by X%: Multiply by (1 - X/100)
